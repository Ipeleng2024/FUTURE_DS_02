# FUTURE_DS_02

# Customer Retention & Churn Analysis

##  Project Overview
This project focuses on analyzing customer retention and churn behavior for a subscription-based business using the Telco Customer Churn dataset.

The goal of the analysis is to identify:
- Why customers leave
- Which customer groups churn the most
- What factors influence customer retention
- How the business can reduce customer churn

This project simulates a real-world business analytics scenario commonly found in SaaS, telecom, fintech, and subscription companies.

---

##  Objectives
The main objectives of this project are to:

- Analyze customer churn trends
- Identify high-risk customer segments
- Evaluate retention drivers
- Understand customer lifetime behavior
- Provide actionable business recommendations

---

##  Key Insights
Some important findings from the analysis include:

- Month-to-month contract customers have the highest churn rate.
- Customers with longer tenure are more likely to stay.
- Customers without tech support churn significantly more.
- Fiber optic customers show higher churn patterns.
- Higher monthly charges are associated with increased churn risk.

---

## Recommendations
Based on the analysis, the following recommendations were made:

- Encourage customers to move to long-term contracts.
- Improve onboarding experience for new customers.
- Promote tech support and security services.
- Create loyalty and retention programs for high-risk customers.
- Review pricing strategies for high-charge customers.
